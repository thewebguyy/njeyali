Frequently Asked Questions

How long does visa processing take?
Visa processing times vary by country and visa type. Tourist visas typically take 5-15 business days, while work and student visas may take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide you with specific timelines for your destination.
What documents do I need for a visa application?
Basic requirements include a valid passport, passport photos, completed application form, and proof of financial means. Additional documents may be required depending on visa type and destination. We'll provide you with a comprehensive checklist.
Can you help with flight changes or cancellations?
Yes! We provide full support for flight changes, cancellations, and rebooking. Contact us as soon as possible, and we'll work with the airline to find the best solution based on your ticket's terms and conditions.
Do you offer travel insurance?
We partner with leading insurance providers to offer comprehensive travel insurance. Coverage includes medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost luggage, and more. We recommend insurance for all international travel.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and mobile money payments. Payment plans are available for packages and corporate bookings. Contact us to discuss payment options that work best for you.
Can I customize a travel package?
Absolutely! All our packages can be customized to match your preferences, budget, and travel style. Our concierge team will work with you to create your perfect itinerary.
